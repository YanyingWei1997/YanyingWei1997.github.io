## Research Interests

- **Carbon Emission Dynamics and Decoupling:** Analyze drivers of regional carbon emissions using LMDI decomposition and Tapio decoupling models, integrating machine learning to optimize carbon peak pathways under uncertainty.
- **Energy Policy Reform and Supply Security:** Examine how electricity market reforms affect energy supply security, with green finance as a policy lens.
- **Regional Corridor Economics and Value Capture:** Study value-capture challenges and hub reconfiguration pathways under China's New International Land-Sea Trade Corridor.
- **Environmental and Natural Resource Economics:** Evaluate land consolidation, ecological-economic coupling, and the economic impact of environmental policies on sustainable development.

My research applies computational and econometric methods to environmental and energy economics, aiming to inform evidence-based policy for sustainable regional development.
